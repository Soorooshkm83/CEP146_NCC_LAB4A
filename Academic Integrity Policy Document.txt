# Seneca College Academic Integrity Policy  

## Purpose  
The purpose of this policy is to uphold academic honesty and ensure fairness in all academic work.  

## Definition  
> **Academic integrity** is the commitment to honesty, trust, fairness, respect, responsibility, and courage in all academic work.  

## Policy Sections  

### 1. General  
- Academic integrity is central to learning and teaching.  
- All members of the Seneca community share responsibility.  

### 2. Academic Integrity Program  
- Provides training and resources for students and faculty.  
- Supports the development of ethical academic practices.  

### 3. Responsibilities  
- **Students:** Complete work honestly and give proper credit.  
- **Faculty:** Educate students about expectations and uphold standards.  
- **Administration:** Provide resources and enforce policy fairly.  

### 4. Sanctions  
- First Offense → Warning or reduced grade  
- Repeated Offense → Failing grade or suspension  
- Serious Offense → Possible expulsion

| Step | Action | Responsibility |
