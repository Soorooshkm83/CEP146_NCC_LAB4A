# Chocolate Chip Cookies  

## Ingredients  
- 2 cups flour  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 1 cup butter  
- 1 tsp baking soda  
- 1 cup chocolate chips  

## Steps  
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).  
2. Mix butter and sugar together until creamy.  
3. Add flour and baking soda, then stir well.  
4. Fold in chocolate chips.  
5. Place spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet.  
6. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.  

**Tip:** Let the cookies cool on the tray for a few minutes before moving them so they don’t fall apart.
